# Authorization for MGC Integration

Integration of MGC into third‑party products or AI agents is free of charge,  
but requires official authorization to ensure ecosystem integrity, security,  
and consistent behavior of the MGC core system.

## What Requires Authorization
- Embedding MGC into any commercial or non-commercial product  
- Integration into AI agents or agent frameworks  
- Distribution of software that bundles MGC  

## What Does Not Require Authorization
- Local personal use  
- Local evaluation or testing  
- Non-distributed internal prototypes

Please contact:  
zkeviny@icloud.com

Provide:  
- Product or agent name  
- Integration purpose  
- Expected distribution scope  
- Contact information  

The MirginCipher Team will respond with authorization confirmation.
